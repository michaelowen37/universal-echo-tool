Echocardiography is a passion of mine. Coupled with a desire to make a positive difference in the lives of patients and sonographers alike, this free echo-tool was born.

It is my goal to always keep this tool free, up-to-date, ad-free and never require or collect any personal information from it's users. That said, as a free tool with thousands of lines of code and countless hours dedicated to building and maintaining, please consider donating. It costs money to host and maintain and all of this and as of now, it is all out of my own pocket.

Anything you can contribute matters, whether it's a one-time or recurring financial contribution or, if you cannot contribute financially, a share with a fellow sonographer, physician, fellow or professor.
