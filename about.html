Welcome!

This is the first echo-specific web app that brings everything you need as a cardiac sonographer together in one place. Designed to be practical in clinical environments, our tool will help you stay up-to-date with the newest sonography guidelines and actually apply them in an effortless way. Whether you're here to implement the overly-dense 2025 diastology updates or you simply wish to calculate an EROA, we've got you covered!

With no login requirements, no ads, no collection of personal information, this tool remains fast, efficient and free. That said, if you find value in this tool, please consider visiting my donate page. Currently I am hosting and maintaining this out of pocket with zero sponsorship (I'm merely a practicing echo tech in the Austin area). I saw a need and simply wished to fill it. Any contributions are greatly appreciated and will certainly encourage me to continue hosting this free tool indefinitely.

My hope is to make echocardiography easy while standardizing practices nation-wide. Feel free to share this tool with your friends, professors and institutions and together, we can improve patient care through quantitative reporting!
