Thank you so much for making it to this page! Echocardiography is a passion of mine and I'm assuming it's one of yours too. Coupled with a desire to make a positive difference in the lives of patients and professionals alike, this free echo-specific tool was born.

It is my goal to keep this tool free, up-to-date, ad-free and never require or collect any personal information ever. That said, as a free tool designed to improve workflows through the simplification of dense algorithms and complex calculations, it costs money.
Built upon thousands of lines of code and countless hours of time, this tool costs money. Maintaining, refining, hosting etc all comes out of my own pocket but to me, it's a cost worth accruing if that's what it takes to improve our industry. However, if you find benefit from this, please consider donating.

Anything you can contribute matters, whether it's a one-time or recurring financial contribution or, if you cannot contribute financially, a share with a fellow sonographer, physician, fellow or professor. Regardless of your level of commitment, I appreciate you reading this far. Together we can make a difference.
